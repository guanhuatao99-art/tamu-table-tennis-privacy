Common questions

How do I register for a tournament?
Create an account or sign in, open the next tournament, and tap Register.

How do I pay the entry fee?
Pay the listed fee via Venmo (link and QR are in the app), then tap “I’ve paid.” An organizer confirms it.

How do I enter my match scores?
You can enter scores for matches you played in; organizers can edit any result.

How do I delete my account?
Open your profile and tap “Delete account.”
